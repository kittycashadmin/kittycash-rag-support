What happens if I miss a payment? | Missing a payment can result in penalties.

How does group voting work? | The admin will poll the voting; the highest number of votes will be the winner.

Can I remove a member from the group? | If a member does not follow the terms and conditions, they should be removed from the group and replaced with a new member.

Where can I find Unique User Id? | Open navigation menu on top left, go to settings to find your UUID.

What is fund target? | This is the amount each member will contribute per term.

What is the Billing Day option for? | This is the date on which contributions will be collected from all members.
Can I add members after creating the group? | Yes, you can invite members anytime by opening the group and selecting the Add Member option.
Can I change group details after creation? | Some settings like group name and profile picture can be changed, but financial terms usually can’t be changed after the first contribution.
How do I make my contribution? | You will get a payment request on the scheduled billing day. You can pay directly from the group page.
Can I be in multiple groups at the same time? | Yes, you can join or create multiple groups, at most 10.

How do I place a bid? | When bidding is active, you will receive a group message. Click the bidding icon and enter your bid amount.
When can I bid? | You can bid only when the bidding status shows in progress. You will get a message in group chat when bidding starts.
How do I know if I won the bid? | The WINNER status will display along with your name in the group chat.
Who can control bidding? | Only the group admin can do.
Can I check the term info? | Yes, Group settings > session settings > terms info.
What are the bidding statuses? | WINNER – The payout for the term has been decided, IN PROGRESS – Bidding is currently open, STOPPED – Bidding is paused by the admin, RESUMED – Bidding has been restarted, FINISHED – The bidding process for the term has ended.
How to know the highest bid while bidding? | While bidding, you can see in the latest bid.
What does Term Frequency mean? | Term Frequency determines how often your group contributes funds—every week, every two weeks, or every month.
How do I select the right Billing Day? | When creating a group, choose a billing day that works best for all members. Recommended: just after salary credit.
How often will I need to contribute funds? | This depends on the Term Frequency (weekly, biweekly, or monthly).
If I pick “Monthly,” will the group be billed on the same day each month? | Yes. Contributions will be collected on the same billing day each month.
Can I change the billing frequency or day after creating the group? | Only before the transactions start you can change the settings.
What does Cashier Selection mean? | Cashier Selection is the process of choosing a group member who will handle the payout for a particular term.
What are the available choices for Cashier Selection? | ADMIN: The group administrator is always the cashier, ADMIN_SELECT: The admin selects a cashier from group members for each term, VOTING: Group members vote to choose the cashier for each term.
Can we change the cashier after group creation? | Yes, the admin can reassign the cashier if needed with group approval.
What is the Payout Process, and why does it matter? | The payout process defines how the group decides who receives the funds for a term.
What are the available payout process types? | RANDOMIZE: chosen randomly among members, BIDDING: Members can bid for the payout, highest bidder receives the fund.
What does 'Allow Multiple Pays/Term' mean? | This setting determines if a member can split their required payment for a term into multiple smaller payments.
What does 'Auto-Pay Required' mean? | If enabled, members must set up automatic recurring payments for their contributions.
What if I select ‘No’ for Auto-Pay Required? | Members can pay manually each term; auto-pay is optional.
Can I change the Auto-Pay requirement after setup? | Yes, you can join if the admin approves, but make sure all payments are completed correctly.
